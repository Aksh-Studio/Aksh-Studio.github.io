Privacy Policy for Aksh Studio

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

Welcome to Aksh Studio. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web applications, including Aksh Calendar, Aksh Calculator, and our Support Hub.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in using Google Authentication, we collect your basic profile information (Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture) to create and secure your workspace environment.
  • App Usage Data: We store the data you explicitly create within our tools, such as Aksh Calendar events, Aksh Calculator history, and Support Hub contribution tokens. This data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Automated & Device Information: We and our third-party advertising partners may collect non-identifiable information such as IP addresses, browser types, and device identifiers to ensure app stability and serve relevant content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Aksh Studio ecosystem.
  • Sync your personal settings (like Dark Mode) and workspace data across devices.
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve our educational and utility tools.
  • Serve personalized advertising via third-party vendors.

3. Third-Party Services & Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display ads and fund our development. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to Aksh Studio and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Alternatively, users can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Google Firebase Analytics & Database

Our backend infrastructure is powered by Google Firebase. Firebase securely encrypts and stores your authentication credentials and calendar/calculator entries. We do not sell your personal database entries to any third parties.

5. Your Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request that we delete your personal workspace data.
  • Modify your consent regarding cookies and targeted advertising using the Privacy & Messaging consent dialog provided on our site.
